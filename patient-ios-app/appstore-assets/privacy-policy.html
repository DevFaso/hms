Privacy Policy

MediHub Patient — iOS App
Last updated: April 3, 2026

1. Introduction

BitnestTechs ("we", "our", "us") operates the MediHub Patient mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal and health information when you use our app.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency and care.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of data, solely for app functionality:

Data TypeExamplesPurpose
Contact InfoName, email, phone numberAccount identification, communication
Health RecordsLab results, vitals, diagnoses, medicationsDisplay your medical information
Clinical DataAppointments, encounters, care team infoAppointment management, visit history
PhotosProfile photoAccount personalization
IdentifiersPatient ID, user IDAuthentication, data retrieval

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Displaying your health records, appointments, and medical information
  • Enabling secure messaging with your care team
  • Managing your patient profile and preferences
  • Authenticating your identity via secure login

4. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell your personal or health data
  • We do not share data with advertisers
  • We do not use your data for analytics or tracking
  • We do not use third-party analytics SDKs
  • We do not collect location data

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections to our secure backend servers (currently hosted at api.hms.dev.bitnesttechs.com). Authentication tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain. We implement enterprise-grade security measures to protect your information.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties except:

  • With your healthcare providers through the hospital's system
  • With family members you explicitly grant access to via the Family Access feature
  • When required by law or legal process

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Revoke family access permissions at any time
  • Contact us to request data deletion

8. Children's Privacy

MediHub Patient is not intended for use by children under 17. We do not knowingly collect data from minors without parental consent.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
