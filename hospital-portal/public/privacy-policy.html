Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 29, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 29, 2026

BitnestTechs ("we", "our", or "us") operates the MediHub Patient mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account credentials – username and password used to authenticate with your healthcare provider's MediHub system.
  • Profile data – name, date of birth, gender, contact details, address, and insurance information as stored in your medical record.
  • Health data – medical records, prescriptions, lab results, vital signs, appointments, billing information, and messages with your care team that you access through the App.
  • User preferences – language selection and notification settings.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information – device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for authentication purposes.
  • Usage data – app feature usage and crash reports to improve app stability.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect precise geolocation data.
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, microphone, or files (unless you explicitly upload a profile photo).
  • We do not serve advertisements or share data with advertising networks.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate you with your healthcare provider's system.
  • Display your medical records, appointments, lab results, prescriptions, and billing information.
  • Enable secure messaging with your care team.
  • Send push notifications for appointment reminders and important health updates (with your consent).
  • Store your language preference for a localized experience.
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs using anonymized crash reports.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • The App communicates with your healthcare provider's MediHub server over encrypted HTTPS connections (TLS 1.2+).
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely using the Android Keystore / iOS Keychain.
  • Your medical data is not stored on the device beyond the current session cache. All data resides on your healthcare provider's servers.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission, secure token storage, and session management.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • With your healthcare provider – The App acts as a portal to your provider's existing medical record system.
  • Crash reporting – Anonymized crash data may be sent to our crash reporting service to improve app stability.
  • Legal requirements – If required by law, regulation, or legal process.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal data through the App.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data through your healthcare provider.
  • Delete your account by contacting your healthcare provider.
  • Opt out of push notifications via your device settings.
  • Change your language preference at any time in the App settings.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services (Android) – for app distribution and updates.
  • Apple App Store (iOS) – for app distribution and updates.

These services have their own privacy policies governing data collection.

8. Data Retention

We retain authentication tokens only for the duration of your active session. Your medical data is managed by your healthcare provider according to their data retention policies and applicable healthcare regulations.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data is processed on servers located in the region where your healthcare provider operates. If data is transferred internationally, appropriate safeguards are applied in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
